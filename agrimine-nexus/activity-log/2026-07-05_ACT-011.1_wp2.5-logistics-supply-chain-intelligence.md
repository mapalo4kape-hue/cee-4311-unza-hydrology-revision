## 4. Key understanding established (cited)
- Zambia is landlocked; freight moves over **regional corridors** (Dar/North-South/Beira/Nacala/Walvis Bay/Lobito), **rail** (Zambia Railways + TAZARA), roads, **OSBPs** (Chirundu et al.), weighbridges, **dry ports** (Kapiri Mposhi), **SEZ/MFEZ** (7 zones), and **fuel** (TAZAMA/INDENI).
- **Customs** runs on **ASYCUDA World** (SAD, risk lanes, Release Order/Exit Note), clearing agents for CIF > USD 2,000, TIP at borders, COMESA/SADC/AfCFTA preferences; imports average **2–7 days** at Chirundu.
- Fleet/warehouse operations are **partly digitised** (GPS, WRS) but largely **paper/manual**; the **same logistics backbone connects all four industries**, with industry-specific specialisations (agri seasonality/cold chain; mining bulk & hazmat; construction site call-offs; environmental chain-of-custody).
