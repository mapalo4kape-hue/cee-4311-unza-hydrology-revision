## 4. Key findings established (cited)
- **Mining:** MRCA No.14/2024 & autonomous MRC; ~19.4% of real GDP; open-pit & underground copper; ASM formalisation via cooperatives + ASM Fund.
- **Construction:** NCC contractor grading (1–6) sets tender ceilings; ZPPA e-GP; local-authority planning permission & occupancy certificates.
- **Environmental:** ZEMA EMA 2011 + EIA SI 3/2026 (Project Brief → screening → EIS → decision letter → monitoring/audit); WARMA water permits; NWASCO utilities.
- **Comparative:** all four industries share a **plan → license → procure → operate → monitor → comply → close** spine, shared entities/documents/assets, overlapping regulators (ZEMA cross-cuts all), and the **same recurring pain points** (manual records, data duplication, approval/payment delays, compliance burden, energy & logistics constraints).
