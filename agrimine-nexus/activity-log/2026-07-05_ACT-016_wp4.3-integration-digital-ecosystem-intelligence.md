## Key findings
- Zambia has a genuine e-gov backbone (ZamPortal + ZamPass + ZamPay + GSB); Mining Cadastre now on the GSB.
- Government access is **portal-first, not API-first**; central-bank/bank/MNO rails are regulated/commercially gated.
- Communication (Africa's Talking) and open geospatial/EO + OSS building blocks are strong and Zambia-applicable; data-exchange standards are mature/open.
