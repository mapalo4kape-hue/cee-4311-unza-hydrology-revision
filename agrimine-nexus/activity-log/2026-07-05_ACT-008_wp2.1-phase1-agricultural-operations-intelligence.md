## 4. Key operational understanding established (cited)
- **Input supply** runs through **FISP e-voucher / ZIAMIS** (register at District Ag Office; K400 farmer + K8,000 govt; SMS code; redeem at accredited agro-dealer with NRC+code; sign GIN) — nationwide 2025/26.
- **Extension** via block/camp network (346 blocks, 1,757 camps) with a stretched ratio up to **1:1,200**.
- **Marketing/storage** via **FRA depots** (pan-territorial maize price) and **ZAMACE certified warehouse-receipt system** (receipts as loan collateral); community aggregation → district warehouses.
- **Contract farming** (cotton/tobacco/sugarcane): inputs-on-credit → buyback with debt deduction; village distributors (15–20 farmers); side-selling is the key risk.
- **Season** Oct–April drives all timing; operations are largely **manual/paper/verbal**, with **mobile money + SMS + ZIAMIS + ZAMACE** as the digital rails.
