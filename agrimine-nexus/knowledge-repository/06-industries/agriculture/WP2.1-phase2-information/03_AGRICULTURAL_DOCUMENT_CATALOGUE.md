# Agricultural Document Catalogue — Zambian Agriculture

> Extends the ACT-008 Operational Document Register with **information content**, **format**, and **retention**. Retention shown where a legal/programme basis exists; otherwise "not standardised (paper)".

## Note
The **legally-structured, retained** documents are the **warehouse receipt** (Agricultural Credits Act 2010; e-WRS + Collateral Registry under the 2026 Act) and **FISP/ZIAMIS records**. The vast majority remain **paper with no standard retention**, recorded as an information-ecosystem gap.
