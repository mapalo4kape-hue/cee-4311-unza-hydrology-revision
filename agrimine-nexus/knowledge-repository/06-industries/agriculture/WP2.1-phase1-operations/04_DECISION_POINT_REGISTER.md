# Decision Point Register — Zambian Agriculture

| ID | Decision | Workflow | Decision-maker | Information used | Timing | Consequence if wrong |
|----|----------|----------|----------------|------------------|--------|----------------------|
| DEC-01 | Which crops/mix to grow | WF-005 | Farmer (+extension) | soil, market prices, rainfall outlook | pre-season | poor fit / low return |

| DEC-03 | Mechanise vs manual land prep | WF-002 | Farmer/manager | area, cost, machine access | Sep–Nov | delayed planting |
| DEC-04 | Soil test or not; lime needed | WF-004 | Farmer/extension | lab access/cost | pre-season | nutrient mismatch |
| DEC-05 | Agro-dealer & input selection | WF-007/008 | Farmer | stock, price, distance, FISP list | Sep–Dec | wrong/late inputs |
| DEC-06 | Planting date & density | WF-011 | Farmer | rainfall onset, labour | Nov–Dec | germination failure |
| DEC-07 | Irrigate / how much | WF-012 | Farmer/operator | soil moisture, forecast, energy cost | dry spells | water/energy waste or stress |
| DEC-08 | Intervene on pest/disease (threshold) | WF-015/016 | Farmer/extension | scouting counts, thresholds | Dec–Apr | yield loss / wasted spray |
| DEC-09 | Chemical vs manual weeding | WF-017 | Farmer | labour cost/availability | Dec–Feb | weed competition |
| DEC-10 | Top-dressing timing & rate | WF-018 | Farmer/extension | growth stage, rain | Dec–Feb | leaching / deficiency |

| DEC-17 | Declare emergency / seek relief | WF-040 | Govt/DMMU/farmer | damage assessment | event | slow response |
| DEC-18 | Grade & consolidate lots | WF-031 | Cooperative/aggregator | quality, moisture, volume | Jun–Sep | rejected lots |

## Note
Most decisions rely on **timely, local information** (rainfall onset, prices, pest thresholds, stock) that is currently fragmented across verbal, SMS, and paper channels.
