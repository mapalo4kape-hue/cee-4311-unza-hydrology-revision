# Stakeholder Operations Handbook — Zambian Agriculture

> For each stakeholder: **role**, key **responsibilities/daily operations**, **tools/systems**, **information produced → consumed**, **pain points**, and the **workflows** they drive. Zambia-specific structures cited.

| Stakeholder | Role & daily operations | Tools/systems | Info produced → consumed | Pain points | Drives workflows |
|-------------|-------------------------|---------------|--------------------------|-------------|------------------|
| **Smallholder farmer** (≤5 ha) | Rain-fed cropping; family labour; FISP inputs; sells to FRA/aggregator/buyer | Phone, mobile money, paper, radio | Field activities, sales → prices, advice, weather | Input timing, price/market access, cash flow, low ratio of extension | WF-002..041 |
| **Commercial farmer** | Larger mechanised operations; own inputs; formal markets/export | Farm records, some ERP/spreadsheets, machinery | Production, sales, compliance → prices, agronomy | Energy/fuel cost, forex, labour, compliance | WF-001..041 |
| **Farm manager** | Plans & supervises operations, labour, machinery, records | Spreadsheets, diaries | Work plans, records → budgets, agronomy | Data capture, labour peaks | WF-002,020,021,023,033,041 |
| **Agronomist** | Technical crop advice; scouting; nutrient/pest plans | Field kit, references | Recommendations → scouting, soil data | Coverage, data access | WF-004,013,015,016,018 |
| **Extension officer (CEO/BEO)** | Camp-level farmer advisory, demos, FFS, data collection (ADEO diary) | ADEO diary, forms, phone | Advice, farmer data → policy, guides | Ratio 1:1,200+; transport; tools [SRC-0049] | WF-013,014,019,025,035,038 |
| **Cooperative** | Aggregates members; distributes inputs (incl. FISP group requirement); bulks output | Membership registers, scales | Membership, aggregated volumes → prices | Governance, trust, working capital | WF-007,008,010,028,031,032,035 |
| **Input supplier (manufacturer/importer)** | Supplies seed/fertilizer/chemicals to dealers | ERP, logistics | Stock, prices → demand | Forex, lead times | WF-007,008,009,010 |
| **Agro-dealer** | Retails inputs; FISP redemption (NRC+code, GIN) via ZIAMIS | ZIAMIS, POS, phone | Sales, redemptions → allocations, stock | Stock-outs, liquidity, accreditation [SRC-0046] | WF-007,008,009 |
| **Transporter** | Moves inputs/produce; rural haulage | Trucks/ox-carts, waybills | Movements → orders, routes | Rainy-season roads, fuel, cost | WF-028 |
| **Warehouse operator** | Stores/grades grain; issues warehouse receipts (WRS) | WRS system, scales, moisture meters | Receipts, stock → grades, prices | Certification, quality, financing [SRC-0051] | WF-026,031 |
| **Commodity buyer / FRA** | Buys grain (FRA pan-territorial price at depots); traders | Depot systems, ZAMACE | Purchases, prices → supply | Payment delays, quality, logistics [SRC-0052] | WF-029,031 |
| **Processor / miller** | Buys grain; processes to meal/products | Plant systems | Demand, offtake → supply, quality | Supply reliability, quality | WF-029 |
| **Exporter** | Aggregates & exports commodities | Trade docs, customs | Export volumes → prices, standards | Corridors, forex, standards | WF-028,029,034 |
| **Bank** | Seasonal credit; WRS-backed loans; payments | Core banking, mobile money | Loans, payments → collateral, WRS | Collateral, smallholder risk | WF-032,037 |
| **Insurance provider** | Crop/weather-index insurance (incl. via FISP) | Policy systems, weather data | Policies, payouts → loss data | Basis risk, data, awareness [SRC-0045] | WF-036 |
| **Government agency (MoA/ZamStats/FRA)** | Policy, FISP, surveys, marketing, statistics | ZIAMIS, survey systems | Programs, statistics → farmer/agro data | Data quality, capacity | WF-025,034,035,040 |
| **NGO / development partner** | Training, aggregation support, resilience programs | Project M&E | Program data → needs | Sustainability, coordination | WF-031,038,040 |
| **Researcher (UNZA/ZARI/IAPRI)** | Agronomy & policy research; variety development | Trials, datasets | Findings, varieties → data | Funding, uptake | WF-004,025 |
| **Equipment dealer** | Sells/services machinery & spares | Dealer systems | Sales, service → demand | Spares, forex, skills | WF-021 |
| **Mechanisation provider** | Hires out tractors/implements (custom hire) | Booking records | Service bookings → demand | Utilisation, breakdowns, access | WF-002,024 |

## Notes
- The **most information-intensive, currently-manual** touchpoints: extension advice, field records, cooperative aggregation, and smallholder marketing.
- The **main existing digital rails**: **ZIAMIS** (FISP registration/redemption), **mobile money** (payments/deposits), **SMS** (codes/advisories), and **ZAMACE** (commodity trading/WRS) [SRC-0045][SRC-0046][SRC-0051].
- Full role SOPs (agro-dealer back office, cooperative governance, warehouse SOPs) require field interviews — recorded as a gap for a later WP2.1 phase.
