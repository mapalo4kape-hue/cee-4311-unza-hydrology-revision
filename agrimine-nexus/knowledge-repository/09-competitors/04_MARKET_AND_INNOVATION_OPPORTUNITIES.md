# Market & Innovation Opportunity Registers (D9–D10)

> Opportunities are **evidence-backed observations of unmet/underserved demand**, tied to feature gaps (GAP-X*) and prior operational research. **They are NOT solution designs, feature specs, or recommendations** — those are out of scope for ACT-011.

| ID | Market opportunity | Evidence basis | Underserved segment |
|----|--------------------|----------------|---------------------|
