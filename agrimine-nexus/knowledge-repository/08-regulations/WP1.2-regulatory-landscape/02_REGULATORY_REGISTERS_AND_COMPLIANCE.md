## Output 2 — Compliance Handbook (how compliance works, operationally)
- **Register/licence first** (mining right, contractor grade, seed/fertiliser registration, data-controller, company/PACRA, cooperative) → **operate within scope** → **report periodically** (production/royalty, tax, environmental, STR, annual returns) → **submit to inspection/audit** (mine safety, ZEMA, labour, customs, procurement) → **renew** on cycle. Cross-cutting duties (data protection, AML, tax, labour, competition) apply to **all** industries.
- **Cross-border** adds customs (ASYCUDA), permits, and preferential-origin compliance (COMESA/SADC/AfCFTA).
- Non-compliance penalties range from fines to licence revocation and imprisonment (per each instrument).
