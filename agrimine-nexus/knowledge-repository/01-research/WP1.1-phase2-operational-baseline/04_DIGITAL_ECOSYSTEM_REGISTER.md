# Digital Ecosystem Register — Zambia

## 1. Mobile & internet
- **Mobile-cellular subscriptions 23.2 million (2024)**, up from 21.1M (2023); projected ~24.5M by end-2025 [SRC-0036] (ZICTA, pub. 2025; confidence 80/100; retrieved 2026-07-05).
- **Internet subscriptions 13.5 million (2024)** (from 12.6M) [SRC-0036].
- **Internet penetration — conflicting figures (recorded):** ZICTA reports ~64.1% (Dec 2023) rising to a claimed 72.1% [SRC-0038] (Freedom House, pub. 2024; confidence 78/100; retrieved 2026-07-05); DataReportal reports **31.2%** (6.51M users) [SRC-0037] (DataReportal, pub. 2024; confidence 70/100; retrieved 2026-07-05). `CONTRADICTS` — methodology differs (subscriptions vs unique users). Logged in Gap Register.
- Mobile connections ~16.40M = 78.7% of population (GSMA via DataReportal) [SRC-0037].
- Over 99% of internet users rely on mobile; fixed-line internet only ~99,000 (Dec 2023) [SRC-0038].

## 2. Digital financial services
- Mobile-money transaction **volumes +33%** in 2024 (values +8%) [SRC-0036]. (Financial-inclusion detail in the Financial Ecosystem Register.)

## 3. Government digital / e-government / digital ID
- Smart Zambia Institute (e-government) and national digital-ID programme — to be catalogued from primary GRZ sources (noted; Gap Register). Governance/data-protection basis established in Phase 1 (Data Protection Act No.3 of 2021; Cyber Security/Crimes Acts 2025).

## 4. Cybersecurity
- Global Cybersecurity Index (GCI) score reported **92.6%**, above global average 65.7% [SRC-0036]. Cyber institutions: ZICTA, national CIRT, Data Protection Commissioner (Phase 1).

## 5. Innovation, universities, research centres
- Universities/research bodies catalogued in Phase 1 Government Organization Register (UNZA, CBU, IAPRI); technology parks/innovation hubs inventory pending (Gap Register).

## 6. Recorded uncertainties (→ Gap Register)
- **Internet-penetration reconciliation** (31.2% vs 64.1%/72.1%) (GAP-111).
- Smartphone vs feature-phone split; computer ownership; digital-literacy metrics (GAP-112).
- E-government service inventory; digital-ID coverage; innovation-hub list (GAP-113).
