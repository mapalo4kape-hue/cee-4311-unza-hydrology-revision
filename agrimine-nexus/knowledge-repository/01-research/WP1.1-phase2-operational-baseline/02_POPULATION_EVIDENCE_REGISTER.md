# Population Evidence Register — Zambia

## 1. National population (2022 Census)
- **19,610,769** (de facto, as of 8 Sep 2022); male **9,603,056**, female **10,007,713** [SRC-0026] (ZamStats, pub. 2023; confidence 95/100; retrieved 2026-07-05).
- **Contradiction recorded:** the Census National Analytical Report / Statistician cite **19,693,423** (de jure basis) [SRC-0004] (ZamStats, pub. 2023; confidence 90/100; retrieved 2026-07-05). Both are ZamStats figures on different bases; logged in Gap Register.
- Rural population exceeds urban (rural ~10.9M; urban ~8.8M) [SRC-0004].
- National population density **26.1 persons/km²** (2022), up from 17.4 (2010) [SRC-0026].

## 3. Households
- **4,056,605 households** (8 Sep 2022); rural 2,339,610, urban 1,716,995 [SRC-0026].
- Average household size **4.8** (rural 5.0, urban 4.6); highest North-Western 5.3, lowest Lusaka 4.5 [SRC-0026].
- LCMS 2022 estimated 3,861,557 households, average size **5.1** [SRC-0027] (ZamStats, pub. 2024; confidence 90/100; retrieved 2026-07-05) — method difference vs census noted.

## 4. Urbanisation, age, gender, education
- Rural/urban split ~55%/45% (rural majority) [SRC-0004].
- More females than males nationally [SRC-0026].
- **Literacy (age 5+) ~62.6%** [SRC-0004]; full age-structure/pyramid and provincial literacy in the Analytical Report (to extract).

## 5. Population growth
- Growth from 13.1M-density-era (2010) to 19.6M (2022); average annual growth ~2.8% (derived from ZamStats 2010 vs 2022) — precise national AAGR to extract from Census tables [SRC-0004] (Gap Register).
