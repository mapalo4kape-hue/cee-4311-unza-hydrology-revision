# Financial Ecosystem Register — Zambia

## 1. Financial inclusion (FinScope 2020)
- Overall financial inclusion **69.4%** (6.6M of 9.5M adults), up from 59.3% (2015) [SRC-0039] (FinScope/FSD Zambia/BoZ, pub. 2020; confidence 86/100; retrieved 2026-07-05).
- **Formal** inclusion **61.3%**; informal 32.3%; **excluded 30.6%** [SRC-0039].
- **Mobile money penetration 58.4%** (from 14.0% in 2015) — the main driver of inclusion [SRC-0039].

## 2. Banking, microfinance, mobile money
- Sector is bank-centric but mobile money dominates uptake [SRC-0039]; mobile-money transaction volumes +33% in 2024 [SRC-0036] (ZICTA, pub. 2025; confidence 80/100; retrieved 2026-07-05).
- Regulator: **Bank of Zambia** (banks & payment systems); **Association of Microfinance Institutions of Zambia** noted [SRC-0039]. (Exact count of licensed commercial banks/MFIs to be taken from the BoZ register — Gap Register.)

## 3. Insurance
- **33 registered insurers**; insurance penetration **1.4% of GDP (2020)** (below African average ~2.78%) [SRC-0040] (PwC/PIA, pub. 2021; confidence 72/100; retrieved 2026-07-05).
- Regulator: Pensions and Insurance Authority (PIA) [SRC-0040].

## 4. Policy & programmes
- National Financial Inclusion Strategy (NFIS) 2017–2022 targeted 80% inclusion / 70% formal by 2022 [SRC-0039]. (Agricultural/mining/SME finance schemes and government financing programmes to be catalogued from BoZ/ministry primaries — Gap Register.)
