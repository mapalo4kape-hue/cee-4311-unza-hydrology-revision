## Output 12 — Geographic Distribution Register
National regulators/ministries in **Lusaka**; mining actors concentrated in **Copperbelt & North-Western**; agriculture across all 10 provinces (rural); construction in urban centres; logistics along **corridors & border districts** (Chirundu, Nakonde, Kasumbalesa, Kazungula); utilities per service area; SEZ/MFEZ at Lusaka, Copperbelt, Chibombo, Kalumbila, Ndola. (Per WP1.1 admin geography + register `geography` column.)

## Output 13 — Digital Maturity Assessment (per register)
- **High:** telecoms/mobile money/fintech/software vendors, ZRA (ASYCUDA), ZPPA (e-GP), banks, ZICTA.
- **Medium:** MoA (ZIAMIS), regulators digitising (PACRA, MRC cadastre, ZamStats), consultants, warehouse operators (WRS).
- **Low:** smallholders, cooperatives, extension officers, waste companies, ASM miners, many contractors — paper/verbal, feature-phone.

## Output 14 — Collaboration Opportunity Register (evidence-observed, not strategy)
Observed points where stakeholders already interconnect (potential collaboration surfaces, **not recommendations**): FISP↔agro-dealers↔mobile money (STK-001/032/062); FRA↔ZAMACE↔banks (WRS finance, STK-026/027/060); ZEMA↔MRC (mining environmental oversight, STK-009/010); ZRA↔C&F↔corridors (trade facilitation, STK-020/056); extension↔research↔farmers (STK-031/072/028); telecoms↔agritech↔farmers (digital advisory, STK-068/073/028).
