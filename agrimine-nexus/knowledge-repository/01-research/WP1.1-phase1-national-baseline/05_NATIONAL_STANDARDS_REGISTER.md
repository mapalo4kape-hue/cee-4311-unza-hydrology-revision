# National Standards Register & National Reference Systems — Zambia

## 1. National standards body
- **Zambia Bureau of Standards (ZABS)** — statutory body under the Ministry of Commerce, Trade & Industry, mandated to develop, publish, maintain, and withdraw Zambian National Standards, under the **Standards Act No. 4 of 2017** [SRC-0016] (GRZ/MCTI, pub. 2017; confidence 88/100; retrieved 2026-07-05).
- **Zambia Weights and Measures Agency (ZWMA)** — legal metrology (noted in the National Industrial Policy) — primary mandate/Act to confirm (Gap Register).
