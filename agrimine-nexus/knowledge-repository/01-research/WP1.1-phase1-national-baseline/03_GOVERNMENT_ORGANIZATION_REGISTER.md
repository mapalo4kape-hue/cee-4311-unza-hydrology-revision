# Government Organization Register — Zambia

> Evidence record. Names/mandates cited to sources; where a legal basis or website is not yet confirmed from a primary source it is marked `(confirm)` and listed in the Gap Register.

## 1. Branches of government
- **Executive:** the President is Head of State; Ministers appointed under Article 116 of the Constitution [SRC-0020] (Cabinet Office, pub. 2025; confidence 85/100; retrieved 2026-07-05). Head of State: H.E. Hakainde Hichilema [SRC-0011] (COMESA, pub. 2024; confidence 82/100; retrieved 2026-07-05).
- **Legislature:** Parliament consists of the President and the **National Assembly**, established by Article 62(1) of the Constitution of Zambia (Amendment) Act No. 2 of 2016 [SRC-0014] (GRZ, pub. 2016; confidence 95/100; retrieved 2026-07-05).
- **Judiciary:** the courts of Zambia (Supreme Court, Constitutional Court, High Court, subordinate courts) — structure per the Constitution [SRC-0014]; detailed court list is a minor gap.

## 4. Public universities & research institutions
- **University of Zambia (UNZA)** — public university; institutional repository at dspace.unza.zm [SRC-0007] (UNZA, pub. 2019; confidence 75/100; retrieved 2026-07-05).
- **Copperbelt University (CBU)** — public university (confirm primary).
- **IAPRI** (Indaba Agricultural Policy Research Institute) — agricultural policy research (see Master Source Register MSR-033).

## 5. Professional & statutory bodies
- **Engineering Institution of Zambia (EIZ)** — statutory professional regulator of engineering, established under **EIZ Act No. 17 of 2010, s.3** [SRC-0021] (EIZ, pub. 2010; confidence 88/100; retrieved 2026-07-05).
- **Zambia Institute of Chartered Accountants (ZICA)** — statutory body for accountants, under the **Accountants Act, 2008** [SRC-0020 synthesis] (confidence 70/100; retrieved 2026-07-05) — primary Act text to confirm.
