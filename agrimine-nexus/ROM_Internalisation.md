## 2. Core research principles (the non-negotiables)

1. **Evidence First** — every conclusion needs evidence. No assumptions, no speculation, no hallucinations, no unsupported recommendations.
2. **Multiple Independent Sources** — validate important findings across Government + Academic + Industry + Practitioner + Company documentation wherever possible.
3. **Source Hierarchy** — rank every source by reliability:

| Tier | What it is |
|------|-----------|
| **Tier 1** | Government publications, Acts, regulations, national & international standards, official statistics, government databases |
| **Tier 2** | Peer-reviewed journals, universities, research institutions, international orgs & development banks (UN, World Bank, FAO, IFAD, ILO, UNEP, WHO, AfDB) |
| **Tier 3** | Industry associations, large consulting firms, annual reports, technical manuals, vendor docs, conference papers, professional societies |
| **Tier 4** | Public discussions, forums, Reddit, blogs, social media — *may reveal pain points, never authoritative without verification* |

---

## 4. Research methodology — the 5-phase lifecycle

1. **Phase 1 — Research Planning:** objectives, scope, exclusions, stakeholders, research/interview/survey questions, datasets, expected outputs.
2. **Phase 2 — Evidence Collection:** documents, regulations, standards, reports, datasets, images, maps, videos, workflows, software screenshots, API docs, manuals, procurement & financial info, org structures, complaints, reviews, feature requests, pricing models, integration docs.
3. **Phase 3 — Validation:** cross-reference findings, compare contradictions, flag outdated info, note regional differences, assess confidence, document uncertainties.
4. **Phase 4 — Knowledge Extraction:** entities, business processes, workflows, KPIs, risks, opportunities, software features, integrations, compliance requirements, AI/GIS/IoT/drone opportunities.
5. **Phase 5 — Product Translation:** convert research into Business / Functional / Non-Functional / System / API / Database / Integration / Security / AI / Analytics / Infrastructure / Testing requirements, plus User Stories and Process Models.
